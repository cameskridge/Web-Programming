  • Youtube Prankster Shot Dead

Youtube pranksters have become a growing subculture of the site for the past handful of years. Their gimmick is to secretly record people and provoke them into giving emotional outbursts. Timothy Wilks, age twenty, thought it would be a good idea to stage a fake robbery with butcher knives in a parking lot. Pretending to rob someone at night clearly turned out to be a bad idea, and now he is dead. The shooter claimed self-defense, and rightfully so. The police are still investigating and have yet to charge him.
