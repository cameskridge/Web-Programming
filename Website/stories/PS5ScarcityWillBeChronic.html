Before The Jump

  • PS5 Scarcity Will Be Chronic

AMD's Ceo Lisa Su offered grim news to those hoping to purchase a PS5 in the near future. Don't count on it. AMD is the company that supplies chips to console manufacturers to make Playstations and Xbox.

This year, AMD launched a new generation of graphics cards for the PC gaming market and has done an awful job on keeping up with demand with most of the supply going to scalpers and miners. Couple that with the increased demand Sony, Microsoft, and those looking to kill time until the pandemic passes, and it becomes clear that the shortage isn't ending anytime soon.

They expect the shortage to last until the end of the year.
